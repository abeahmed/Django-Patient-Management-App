The story:

The idea for Paegex came about when I was
tasked with finding a patient management software,
my mother could implement in her clinic.
I experimented using various different software,
but none of them seemed to fit.
They weren't very user-friendly and,
many of them, too complex.
It was then that I decided to create
a patient management system of my own,
one with a simplified interface,
one which is easy to use, and most importantly,
one which is secure.
And so, Paegex was born.
